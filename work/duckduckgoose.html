Unity Game

Duck Duck Goose

Contribution:

     This was the first game I made while in my 1st year at RRC Game Development Programming course. I worked solo on this game and designed everything based on an Atari-style game. I based mine on Asteroids and created a similar mechanic to asteroids with my own silly twist. game. I created all the art aspects of the game as well as designed the UI. I programmed a simple scoring system and a health-based life system. I also added in some lily pads as obstcales and created some collision detection.

Game Summary:

     You play as a goose swimming in a pond, while it's increasingly getting infested with ducks. Your mission is to save your little home and shoot as many ducks as possible with your poo.
