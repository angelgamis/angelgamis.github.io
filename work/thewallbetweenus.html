The Wall Between Us

Contribution:

The Wall Between Us is a game I made in grade 12 in a program called TechHub. I worked on this game solo with pre-exsisting art assets used from Itch.io. I worked on the game mechanic of shooting a magical ability to shoot vines in a 360 area of the player as well as the movement of the player. I also programmed the mechanic of being able to climb the vines to make your way through the labirinth.
