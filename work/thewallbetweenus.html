Game Summary:

     You play as a magical being who got was cursed by and evil sorcerer to seperate you from the love of your life. The curse caused harmful creatures to spawn in between the two of you. In the heat of the moment your magical partner decided to use their magical power of earth, to build a labrith around your home village to protect everyone inside. You are now lost far away from your home and need to get back. So you use your magical ability of plants to navigate the labrith and help you climb steep walls and terrain. Make your way back home and avoid dangers along the way!
