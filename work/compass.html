Unity Game

Compass

Contribution:

     Compass is the first group video game I had ever worked on and my first steps into learning game development and C#. I worked on this game while in Techhub in 2021. This was a group collaboration where everyone worked on one NPC enemy and programmed and did the art for their enemy. I designed a gargoyle statue that would chase and attack the player when it gets within a certain distance from it. I did the art and animations of the gargoyle and programmed its mechanic. I also worked on the players sword mechanic as an extra task. I also created and designed the starting screen UI. This game was left a work in progress but is a great peice to show where I started along my game development journey.

Game Summary:

     Locked in a dungeon you must defeat all 6 bosses to escape. Use your sword and magical abilities to slay all the monsters in each portal room to win the game!
